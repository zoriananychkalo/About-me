01 — About

A little about me

My name is Zoriana and I am just getting started in software development. I will not pretend I know everything — I am here to learn, build things, break them, and figure out how they work. I like the feeling of understanding something that confused me the day before.

Outside of code I spend most of my time with plants, music, food, and a volleyball. I think curiosity is the most useful skill anyone can have, in tech or anywhere else.

02 — Hobbies

Things I love doing

Plants

I have more plants than I probably should. There is something calming about keeping things alive and watching them grow slowly.

Volleyball

The teamwork, the quick decisions, the energy of a good rally — this is where I feel most alive.

Guitar

I play guitar when I need a break from screens. Still learning, still getting calluses, still loving it.

Music

Music is on almost all the time — it changes with my mood and somehow always fits.

Photography

I like capturing small moments. A plant in the right light, a corner of a street, something nobody else stopped to look at.

Cooking

Cooking is my other creative outlet. I experiment a lot and enjoy feeding people even more than eating myself.

A dream — surfing

One day I really want to try surfing. There is something about riding a wave that sounds like the most freeing thing in the world. The only small issue is that I do not know how to swim yet — but I figure that is just step one of the plan.

03 — Dreams

Things I want to do

I want to learn motion design — the way things move and breathe on screen has always caught my eye, and I would love to be the one creating that.

I also want to travel, properly. Not just visit places but really capture them — with a camera, with video, with all the light and noise and stillness that makes a place feel alive. I imagine putting together films of the places I go and the nature I find along the way. That is the kind of project I want to build one day.
